About Me

I'm a final-year Software Engineering student at Daffodil International University, currently working toward my B.Sc. with a CGPA of 3.87/4.00. Alongside coursework, I spend a lot of my time teaching — tutoring students one-on-one in programming and networking, and posting CS explainer videos on YouTube for anyone trying to make sense of the same topics I once found confusing.

Research Focus

ONGOING · 2026

Multi-Label Emotion Classification in Bangla Text

Extending existing single-label emotion corpora in Bangla into multi-label datasets, then benchmarking a range of machine learning approaches against them. Bangla is a widely-spoken but under-resourced language in NLP — this thesis aims to close part of that gap and provide a foundation for richer emotion-aware applications in Bengali.
