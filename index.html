Research Focus

ONGOING · 2026

Multi-Label Emotion Classification in Bangla Text

Extending existing single-label emotion corpora in Bangla into multi-label datasets, then benchmarking a range of machine learning approaches against them. Bangla is a widely-spoken but under-resourced language in NLP — this thesis aims to close part of that gap and provide a foundation for richer emotion-aware applications in Bengali.
